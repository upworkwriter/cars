Frequently Asked Questions

Can I choose a specific car model when booking my rental?
Most car rental services allow you to select a car category (e.g., compact, SUV, luxury), but not a specific make or model. Your actual vehicle will be based on availability at the time of pickup.
Is it possible to upgrade my vehicle class at the time of pickup?
Yes, upgrades are often possible at the time of pickup depending on inventory. However, they typically come with an additional cost which varies by location and vehicle class.
What are the requirements for renting higher-end or luxury vehicles?
Renting luxury vehicles usually requires meeting higher age restrictions, having a valid drivers license with a clean driving record, and presenting a credit card with sufficient available credit for a larger security deposit.
Are there any long-term benefits or rewards programs for frequent renters that could offer flexibility with vehicle choices?
Yes, many car rental companies have loyalty programs that reward frequent renters with points that can be used for free rentals or upgrades, as well as other perks such as priority service and discounted rates.
