Frequently Asked Questions

What factors should I consider when choosing a rental car?
When choosing a rental car, you should consider the type of vehicle that suits your needs (size, capacity, fuel efficiency), the cost and what it includes (insurance, mileage, additional fees), and the rental companys reputation and policies (cancellation policy, customer service). Also assess any extras you may need such as GPS, child seats or additional drivers.
How can I get the best deal on a car rental?
To get the best deal on a car rental, book in advance as prices tend to rise closer to your pick-up date. Compare prices from different companies online using aggregator websites. Check for discounts through membership programs or promotional codes. Avoid airport rentals if possible since they often carry premium fees. Consider renting for longer periods like a week to benefit from lower daily rates.
What do I need to know about insurance coverage for my rental car?
You need to understand what types of insurance are offered by the rental company such as Collision Damage Waiver (CDW), Theft Protection (TP), Personal Accident Insurance (PAI), and Supplemental Liability Insurance (SLI). Determine if your personal auto insurance covers rentals or if your credit card provides any rental car insurance benefits. Choose the coverage that ensures you are fully protected without paying for redundant coverage. Always read the fine print and ask questions if anything is unclear regarding the insurance policy before finalizing your rental agreement.
