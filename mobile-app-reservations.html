Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a car using the mobile app?
To reserve a car using the mobile app, download the car rental services app from your smartphones app store, create an account or log in, select your desired rental dates and location, choose your preferred vehicle category, review the price and terms, add any additional services or coverage if needed, and then confirm your reservation by providing payment details.
What documents do I need to present when picking up a rental car reserved through the mobile app?
When you arrive at the pickup location for your reserved rental car, youll typically need to present a valid drivers license that matches the name on the reservation. Depending on the policy of the car rental company and local laws, you may also be required to show additional identification such as a passport or credit card used for booking. Make sure to check with the specific rental agency for their requirements beforehand.
Can I modify or cancel my reservation through the mobile app after it has been made?
Yes, most car rental services allow you to modify or cancel your reservation directly through their mobile apps. However, cancellation policies vary by company and may depend on how close to the pickup date you are trying to cancel. It is important to review these policies before making changes; some reservations may incur fees for modifications or cancellations especially if they are done last minute.
