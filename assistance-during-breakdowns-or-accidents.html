Frequently Asked Questions

What assistance is provided by the car rental service in case of a breakdown or accident?
Car rental services typically offer 24/7 roadside assistance for breakdowns, which includes services like towing, jump-starts, tire changes, and lockout assistance. In case of an accident, they can help facilitate insurance claims, provide a replacement vehicle if needed, and give guidance on local procedures for reporting accidents to authorities.
Is there an additional fee for roadside assistance or accident support?
Roadside assistance may be included in the rental agreement or available as an optional service with a separate fee. Accident support is typically covered by the insurance policy that comes with the rental; however, charges could apply depending on the type of coverage selected and the circumstances of the accident.
What steps should I take immediately after an accident in a rented car?
Firstly, ensure everyones safety and call emergency services if there are injuries. Then contact the rental companys emergency number to report the incident; they will guide you through their specific process. Exchange information with any other parties involved and take photos of the scene for documentation. Do not admit fault or make any statements that could affect an insurance claim without consulting your rental agency first.
