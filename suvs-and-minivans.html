What is the difference in space and seating capacity between SUVs and minivans offered in your rental services?
SUVs typically offer seating for 5-7 passengers with variable cargo space depending on the size class (compact, mid-size, or full-size). Minivans generally provide seating for 7-8 passengers with more consistent, ample cargo space behind the third-row seats due to their boxier design. Both usually have foldable rear seats for additional cargo room.
Are there additional costs associated with renting an SUV or minivan compared to standard car models?
Yes, SUVs and minivans are often more expensive to rent than standard cars due to their larger size, greater passenger capacity, and higher demand for family travel or group trips. The price can also be influenced by factors such as rental duration, location, insurance coverage options, and seasonal demand.
Can child safety seats be provided when renting an SUV or minivan from your service?
Most car rental services offer child safety seats upon request for an additional fee. Its advisable to reserve these items in advance to ensure availability. Youll need to specify the age and weight of the children so that an appropriate seat can be provided according to local safety regulations.
