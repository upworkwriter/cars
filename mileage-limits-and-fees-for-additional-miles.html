Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard mileage limit for car rentals?
The standard mileage limit for car rentals varies by company and rental agreement, but many companies offer a daily limit between 100-250 miles. Some may provide unlimited mileage, especially for longer-term rentals or specific vehicle categories.
Are there fees for exceeding the mileage limit?
Yes, if you exceed the agreed-upon mileage limit, rental companies will charge additional fees. These overage charges are typically calculated on a per-mile basis and can be quite costly.
How much do rental companies charge for additional miles?
The cost of additional miles can range from $0.10 to $0.25 or more per mile beyond the set limit. The exact rate depends on the rental companys policies and the type of vehicle rented.
Can I purchase extra miles in advance, and is it cheaper than paying for overages later?
Many car rental services offer the option to buy extra miles in advance at a discounted rate compared to the per-mile fee charged after surpassing the limit. This pre-purchase can be significantly cheaper and provide peace of mind if you know your trip will exceed the standard mileage cap.
