Frequently Asked Questions

What is a one-way rental in car services?
A one-way rental in car services refers to renting a vehicle from one location and returning it to a different location. It offers flexibility for travelers who are not planning to return to the same starting point.
Are there additional charges for one-way rentals?
Yes, most car rental companies impose an additional fee for one-way rentals known as a drop-off charge. This fee compensates for the potential cost of returning the vehicle to its original location or other logistical expenses related to the imbalance of cars at their locations.
How can I find out the drop-off charge for my one-way rental?
The drop-off charge should be disclosed at the time of booking your car rental. You can find this information on the companys website, by contacting customer service directly, or reviewing your reservation details if youve already booked. The specific amount can vary based on several factors including distance between locations, popularity of the route, and seasonal demand.
