Frequently Asked Questions

What are early bird discounts and how can I qualify for them when renting a car?
Early bird discounts are special reduced rates offered to customers who book their car rentals well in advance. To qualify, you typically need to reserve the vehicle a specified number of days or weeks before the pick-up date. The exact time frame varies by rental company, but its often around 30 to 60 days ahead.
Do car rental companies offer last-minute deals, and if so, how do I find them?
Yes, many car rental companies provide last-minute deals to fill up their inventory that would otherwise go unused. You can find these deals by checking the companys website, signing up for newsletters, using their mobile app, or searching through travel deal websites shortly before or on your intended day of rental.
Are there any restrictions or drawbacks associated with early bird discounts or last-minute deals on car rentals?
Restrictions may apply such as blackout dates during peak seasons when discounts arent available. Drawbacks include limited selection of vehicles for last-minute deals and potential fees for changes or cancellations with early bird reservations. Always read the terms and conditions carefully before booking to understand any limitations or penalties.
