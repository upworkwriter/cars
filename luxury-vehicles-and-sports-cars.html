Frequently Asked Questions

What types of luxury vehicles and sports cars are available for rent?
Car rental services typically offer a range of luxury vehicles and sports cars including brands like Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi, Porsche, Ferrari, Lamborghini, Aston Martin, and more. The specific models available will vary depending on the rental companys fleet.
How much does it cost to rent a luxury or sports car?
The cost to rent a luxury or sports car varies greatly depending on the make and model, rental duration, location, and time of year. Prices can range from a few hundred dollars per day for entry-level luxury cars to several thousand dollars per day for high-end sports cars.
Are there any special requirements for renting a luxury vehicle or sports car?
Yes. Rental companies often have stricter requirements for renting high-end vehicles. These may include a higher minimum age (usually 25 or 30), a valid drivers license with a clean driving record, increased security deposits, and comprehensive insurance coverage.
Can I rent a luxury vehicle or sports car for an extended period or take it on a long-distance trip?
This depends on the rental services policies. Some companies may allow long-term rentals or long-distance trips but could impose mileage limits and require advance notice. Additional fees might apply if you exceed the allotted mileage or wish to travel out-of-state or internationally. Always check with the rental provider beforehand regarding their specific terms and conditions.
