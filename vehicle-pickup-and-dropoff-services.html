Frequently Asked Questions

Are there additional fees associated with vehicle pickup and dropoff services in car rental services?
Yes, many car rental companies charge additional fees for the convenience of vehicle pickup and dropoff services. These fees vary depending on factors like the distance from the rental office to the requested location, time of day (after-hours may incur extra charges), and level of service requested (e.g., curbside delivery versus delivery within a parking structure).
Can I change my designated pickup or dropoff location after making a reservation with a car rental service?
Changes to your designated pickup or dropoff location might be possible but are subject to availability and could incur additional charges. It is important to contact the car rental company as soon as possible to request changes. Some companies might offer flexibility within a certain timeframe before scheduled service; however policies vary by company so its essential to review their specific terms or talk directly with customer service for guidance.
